Bioinforum'22

13 June 2022



Bioinforum, an engaging and dynamic event held on June 13th, 2022, provided a vibrant platform for students to immerse themselves in a plethora of activities and intellectual pursuits within the realm of biosciences. With an enthusiastic participation from numerous students, the event buzzed with energy and excitement as participants delved into various competitions and interactive sessions. Through a blend of learning and enjoyment, participants not only enriched their knowledge but also created lasting memories that would resonate far beyond the event itself, embodying the essence of discovery and camaraderie in the world of biosciences. The 4th edition of the GEN(E)IUS magazine was also released on this day.
