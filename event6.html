PRATYARTH'20

Symposium on Data Analytics in Bioinformatics, 7th March, 2020



Event Details:

Pratyarth is an annual symposium on seminal research in bioinformatics organised by COMBIGS, the Computational Biology Group at SASTRA Deemed University. This year we bring you an open arena of brainstorming minds from different technical domains to enrich us in fields like Network Biology, QSAR analysis, Data Science in Biology and surprisingly we also have a power talk session from a like minded person.


SPEAKERS:


Dr. Subramanian V
Cheif Scientist, CSIR-Central Leather Research Institute, Chennai

Dr. V Subramanian's is a specialized scientist in Inorganic & Physical Chemistry. His areas of expertise are deep in Applications of molecular dynamics and molecular mechancs, QSAR and biological activity/toxicity prediction, Design of novel functional materials, Nanomaterials and Engineering of proteins and metalloproteins.


Dr. Himanshu Sinha
Associate Professor, Dept. of Biotechnology, IIT-M, Chennai

Dr. Himanshu Sinha is an Associate Professor from Department of Biotechnology, IIT - M, Chennai. He heads a Systems Genetics Laboratory at IIT-M. His areas of interest are Population and Quantitative Genetics, Network biology and interactomics. He obtained his doctoral degree from the University of Cambridge, UK in Plant Sciences


Dr. Shubhada Hegde
DST-INSPIRE Faculty Scientist |Network Biology, IBAB, Bengaluru

Dr. Shubhada Hegde is an INSPIRE Faculty Scientist at Institute of Bioinformatics and Applied Biotechnology, Bengaluru. Her research areas are genomic analysis, network analysis and system biology. She received her Ph.D. in Computational Systems Biology and Engineering from Centre for DNA fingerprinting and Diagnostics.


Mr. Vijay Anand G
Founder - Managing Director Point One Promotions, Chennai

Mr. Vijay Anand is one among the young entrepreneurs who found Point One Promotions while pursuing undergraduate and now operates full time in the industry of Digital Marketing and Operations. He worked in Mercedes-Benz, Chennai and later on went onto make his startup into a full-time organization with a bunch of clients.


Dr. Shanthi B
Associate Dean - Research, SASTRA Deemed University, Thanjavur

Dr. Shanthi brings an extensive background in Big Data analytics, Image processing, ML and AI. She has published more than 100 papers in peer reviewed journals and has completed 2 funded projects in ML and Image analysis techniques. She was the Associate Dean - Research in School of Computing, SASTRA Deemed University Thanjavur.
