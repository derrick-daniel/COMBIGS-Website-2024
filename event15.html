Launch of COMBIGS website

16th November 2022



Event Details:

The inauguration and launch event of the COMBIGS website for the term 2022-2023, held on November 16th, 2022, marked a significant milestone in the COMBIGS's journey. Led by esteemed Chief Guest, Prof. P. Chellapandi, the event witnessed the ceremonial inauguration of the newly elected office bearers for the term. Alongside, the occasion commemorated the unveiling of the freshly designed and updated COMBIGS website, symbolizing a commitment to innovation and accessibility in the realm of bioinformatics and genome sciences. A highlight of the event was a thought-provoking talk delivered by Chief Guest, Dr. Chellapandi, representing the Department of Bioinformatics at the School of Life Sciences, Bharathidasan University. The insightful discourse shed light on emerging trends and challenges in the field, inspiring attendees to explore new frontiers of knowledge. Additionally, the event was graced by the presence of Associate Dean, Dr. Ponnusami, who extended recognition and appreciation to the distinguished guests and contributors, underscoring the collaborative spirit and dedication driving COMBIGS forward.

Cheif Guest:

Dr. Chellapandi - Department of Bioinformatics, School of Life Sciences, Bharathidasan University
